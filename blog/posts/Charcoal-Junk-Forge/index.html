Blacksmith's Forge Built From Junk

A simple blacksmith's forge can be made from just about anything.

We helped clean out a garage and rescued a bunch of junk from being thrown away.

In this build, a leaf blower, an old car wheel, a section of steel railing and some empty propane torch tanks come together to create a functional forge suitable for forging small items.

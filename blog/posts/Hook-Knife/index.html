Upcycled Bowl Carving Knife

Here's another upcycled knife for you. Once again, I used some wood from my stash of walnut for the handle and an old saw blade served as the stock for the blade.

The process of making this knife is very similar to my carving knives and my survival machete. Unlike my previous knives, I didn't hold the blade in place with pins but instead used a ferrule made from a piece of copper pipe.

Check out the video to get a look at the entire process!
